Create & use a Logical Volume

Create playbook /home/admin/ansible/ansible/lv.yml that runs on all managed nodes and does the following:

  • Create a logical volume in volume group research
  • Logical volume name: data
  • Requested size: 1500 MiB
  • Format the logical volume with ext4
  • If requested size cannot be created, display message: Could not create logical volume of that size and use 800 MiB instead
  • If volume group research does not exist, display: volume group does not exist
  • Do not mount the logical volume
